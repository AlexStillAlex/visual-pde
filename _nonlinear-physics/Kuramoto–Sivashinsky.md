We now study the [Kuramoto–Sivashinsky equation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kuramoto%E2%80%93Sivashinsky_equation),

$$\pd{u}{t} = -\nabla^2u-\nabla^4u-|\nabla u|,$$

with periodic boundary conditions.
